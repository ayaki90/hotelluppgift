About us

About – Elysian Heights Hotel Welcome to Elysian Heights Hotel, where modern luxury meets the warmth of a true home away from home. Nestled in the heart of Dubai, our hotel is designed for travelers who seek comfort, elegance, and unforgettable experiences.

At Elysian Heights, we believe that true luxury is not only seen — it is felt. Every detail, from our serene interior design to our thoughtfully curated amenities, is crafted to bring you a sense of calm, belonging, and effortless sophistication.

Our philosophy is simple: you deserve to feel at home, even when you’re far from it. That’s why every room and suite combines residential comfort with contemporary design, inviting you to rest, recharge, and indulge at your own pace.

We are deeply committed to exceptional service. Our dedicated team — trained to anticipate your needs before you even voice them — is passionate about delivering world-class hospitality with a personal touch. Whether you’re here for business, leisure, or celebration, we strive to create meaningful moments that stay with you long after your visit.

From breathtaking rooftop views and fine dining experiences to rejuvenating spa retreats and vibrant social spaces, Elysian Heights Hotel is more than a destination — it’s an experience crafted for the modern traveler.

At Elysian Heights, your journey becomes a memory, and every stay feels like coming home.

Room Features & Amenities

All our rooms are designed to deliver exceptional comfort and luxury. Every room category includes a private bathroom equipped with both a bathtub and a rainfall shower for a relaxing, spa-inspired experience. Our Suites and Presidential Suites offer elevated indulgence, featuring a spacious master bathroom, a beautifully furnished living area, and access to a private sauna. These room types also include an in-room 'jacuzzi for the ultimate sense of relaxation. Our Standard Rooms provide all essential comforts in an elegant and refined setting, featuring a modern bathroom with a bathtub and rainfall shower — but without jacuzzi features.
